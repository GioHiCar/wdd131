Site Name:

BBall-Bae.org is the domain. The original name idea was "Basketball-Girlfriend" because the intended audience is people who are trying to learn about the NBA; which in most cases would be a girl who's partner watches it. Bae is just another term for girlfriend and makes the name fun to say.

Site Purpose:

Inform new NBA viewers about the NBA like the 32 teams and their players, where to stream games, and an overview of the organization's history
